Robotica 2015

Designing visuals for the Robitca 2025 festival.

Intro

Robotica 2025 is a festival about different robots. The goal is to teach people about the impact of robots.


Challenges

To design a visual that invites the user to come and join the festival.


Goal

The visual of the website should be interesting for everyone, there is no strict target: everyone should be involved.

Roles

UX/UI designer


Deliverables

Sketches (a lot), style tile and visual designs.


Tools

Adobe's Illustrator and Photoshop, pen and paper

Sketches

Sketching helps my creativity to flow. There is no such thing as right or wrong.

style tile

A style tile guides me through the designing process. Experimenting with the colors is important, I chose blue in combination with orange. There is a lot of contrast between these two colors, making them both noticeable.

wireframing

Making wireframes helps me to place the elements and to try different options. Testing this and processing the feedback is the base of a good visual design.

end result: the visuals

Using Photoshop and Illustrator, I was able to visualise the webpage. I chose to make a one pager.
