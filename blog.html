Blog.

I made myself a promise: I will travel to different countries to learn about different cultures. The fact that we have more cultures than countries is just amazing to me. The reason why I'm interested in this subject, is because we make choices based on our experiences. It shows that you have thought about the actions you take or the way you think about something. Experience gives you a good opportunity to gain useful skills. I want to work with a range of people from all sorts of different backgrounds.

Okay, that sounds good, but what do you want to achieve? Great question, let me explain: everybody is used to a certain type of patterns. It depends on the place you live in. Facebook for example, is a platform with different layouts for different countries. Behind every design, there's an explanation why. To achieve great user experience, you need to learn about the differences and the preferences. I would love to blog about the things I learn during my journey. This keeps me motivated and makes me realise more things.
